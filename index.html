Observation & Conclusion

The data from the past week shows a clear trend: short-form video content is driving significantly higher engagement and click-through rates compared to static infographics. Our total views increased by 15%, but the CTR on our latest infographic was 0.8% lower than the weekly average. This suggests our audience is more receptive to video content in their social feeds.

Recommendations

We should prioritize the creation and promotion of short-form video content. While infographics are valuable, they should be used to support video content rather than as standalone posts.
